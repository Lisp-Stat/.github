## How to Sign

When you open a pull request for the first time, the CLA bot will comment on it.  Post the following comment on your pull request, replacing the placeholder values:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
Email: your.email@example.com
Age: your-age
Signing as: I am signing on behalf of myself.
```

Or if signing on behalf of an employer:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
Email: your.email@example.com
Age: your-age
Signing as: I am signing on behalf of my employer.
```

You only need to sign once across all Lisp-Stat repositories.
